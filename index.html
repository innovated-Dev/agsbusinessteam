Real Estate & Property

How much does a real estate website cost?

At AGS (AGELESS) Business Agency, real estate website packages start from ₦500,000 in Nigeria, £800 in the UK, and $1,000 in the USA for a clean responsive property listing site. Full-scale platforms with agent profiles, advanced search filters, lead capture, and CRM integration range from ₦850,000 / £2,500 / $3,000 depending on complexity. All projects are quoted in your local currency.

Can AGS (AGELESS) help my property listings rank on Google?

Yes. We build every real estate website with technical SEO built in — fast load speeds, structured data markup, keyword-optimised property pages, and local SEO targeting your city. We have helped estate agents in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, UK, and USA rank for high-intent searches like "houses for sale in Lagos" and "real estate agent near me."

What features should a professional real estate website have?

A high-converting real estate website needs: advanced property search with filters, high-resolution photo galleries, agent profile pages, a lead capture form, WhatsApp or live chat integration, Google Maps, mobile-first responsive design, fast loading speed, and SEO-optimised listing pages. AGS (AGELESS) builds all of this as standard, not as extras.

How do I get more leads from my real estate website?

Most estate agency websites lose leads because of slow load times, poor mobile experience, and no clear call-to-action. AGS (AGELESS) fixes all three — we optimise your site speed, redesign your conversion flow, and add strategic lead capture points so visitors become enquiries. We also offer social media marketing and Google Ads management to drive targeted traffic to your listings.

Do you create real estate marketing materials like flyers and property posters?

Yes. Our graphic design team creates scroll-stopping property flyers, listing posters, social media creatives, and branded marketing materials for estate agents and property developers. Everything is designed to make your listings stand out instantly — both online and in print.

Ecommerce & Business Owners

How much does it cost to build an ecommerce store?

AGS (AGELESS) builds ecommerce stores starting from ₦650,000 in Nigeria, £1,000 in the UK, and $1,200 in the USA for a WooCommerce or custom storefront with product pages, cart, and checkout. Full Shopify stores with custom design, payment integration, and inventory management start from ₦1,000,000 / £2,800 / $3,500. All projects are quoted in your local currency.

Can you help me get more sales from my online store?

Yes. We specialise in ecommerce conversion optimisation — improving product pages, checkout flow, page speed, and mobile experience to turn more visitors into buyers. We also run Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, Google Shopping campaigns, and email marketing sequences designed specifically to drive ecommerce revenue.

What payment methods can you integrate for a Nigerian ecommerce store?

We integrate all major Nigerian and international payment gateways including Paystack, Flutterwave, Stripe, PayPal, and bank transfer options. For UK and US stores we integrate Stripe, Square, and Shopify Payments. We also set up secure SSL certificates and ensure full PCI compliance for every store we build.

Can AGS (AGELESS) manage my social media and digital marketing too?

Absolutely. AGS (AGELESS) is an all-in-one agency — we do not just build your store and leave. We offer full social media management, content creation, paid advertising, SEO, and email marketing so your ecommerce brand keeps growing after launch. One team handles everything, which means consistent branding and no coordination headaches.

How long does it take to build and launch an ecommerce website?

A standard ecommerce store takes 2 to 4 weeks from brief to launch. Complex platforms with custom features, multiple product categories, and third-party integrations take 4 to 8 weeks. We provide a clear project timeline upfront so you always know what is happening and when your store goes live.

Authors & Creators

Do I need a website as an author?

Yes — your author website is your most powerful long-term asset. Social media algorithms change, but your website belongs to you permanently. It is where readers discover your books, join your mailing list, buy directly from you, and connect with your brand. AGS (AGELESS) builds author websites that look professional, load fast, and are optimised to grow your readership organically through SEO.

Can you help me sell my books directly from my website?

Yes. We build author websites with direct book sales built in — physical copies, digital downloads, or both. We integrate payment gateways so readers can purchase without leaving your site, keeping 100% of the revenue with you instead of giving a cut to Amazon or third-party retailers. We also set up email list integration so every buyer becomes a long-term fan.

Can AGS (AGELESS) design my book cover and author brand?

Yes. Our graphic design team creates professional book covers, author logos, brand colour palettes, social media kits, and promotional materials. A strong visual brand makes your books look credible and professional whether they appear on Amazon, in bookstores, or on social media — and it makes readers trust you before they even read a word.

How do I promote my book launch online?

AGS (AGELESS) runs full book launch campaigns covering social media marketing, email sequences, paid advertising on Facebook and Instagram, influencer outreach, and content strategy. We create the buzz before launch, drive sales during launch week, and build your ongoing readership after. We have helped authors grow from zero to thousands of engaged followers within months.

How much does an author website cost?

Author websites at AGS (AGELESS) start from ₦300,000 in Nigeria, £700 in the UK, and $900 in the USA for a professional site with book showcase, about section, blog, and contact form. Full author platforms with direct book sales, email list integration, and reader community features start from ₦500,000 / £2,000 / $2,500. All projects are quoted in your local currency with no hidden fees.
